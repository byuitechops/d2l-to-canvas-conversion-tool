    14683 Introduction to the Humanities. Students will examine key works in the arts from Renaissance through the early 20th Century, their historical and cultural context, and the artistic principles that contribute to their significance. Students will then apply these principles in their own creative work, and in making value assessments about the arts. Topics covered in this course include elements of visual art, music, theatre, literature, cinema; works of art within their historical periods and styles.
